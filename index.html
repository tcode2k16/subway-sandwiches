Sandwiches


Have you ever wondered how many unique subway sandwiches you can make? Today we are going to find out.



Start with the bread


According to the subway website, you have six types of bread to pick from:

  • Italian Wheat
  • Hearty Italian
  • Honey Oat
  • Parmesan Oregano
  • Multigrain
  • Flatbread

This gives us 6 options to start with.



Add the Filling


You can choose one of the 16 different fillings:

  • Chicken Bacon Ranch
  • Chicken Teriyaki
  • Cold Cut Trio
  • Egg Mayo
  • Italian B.M.T.™
  • Meatball Marinara Melt
  • Roast Beef
  • Roasted Chicken Breast
  • Steak & Cheese
  • Subway Club™
  • Chicken Ham
  • Subway Melt™
  • Tuna
  • Turkey
  • Veggie Delite™
  • Veggie Patty

Now we have more options:

$$6 \times 16 = 96$$



Time for vegetables


You can also choose to add any of the 8 vegetables:

  • Cucumbers
  • Green Bell Peppers
  • Lettuce
  • Red Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Black Olives
  • Jalapenos
  • Pickles

This expands the number of options by a lot:

$$6 \times 16 \times 2^8 = 24576$$



Finish off with some sauces


Pick one of the 6 sauces available:

  • Chipotle Southwest
  • Ranch
  • BBQ
  • Chili Sauce
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mayonnaise

Now we have more options:

$$6 \times 16 \times 2^8 \times 6 = 147456$$



Last but not least...


You also have some extra things to add on:

  • Processed Cheddar
  • Monterey Cheddar
  • Extra cheese
  • Toast the beard
  • 6-inch or footlong

Now, the number of options equals:

$$6 \times 16 \times 2^8 \times 6 \times 2^5 = 4718592$$



Summary


As you can see, there are more than 4,718,592 possible combinations.


So the next time you walk into a subway, try a different sandwich.
